<a id="backlinks-spam"></a>
## Backlinks and spam

Provider metrics are source-specific. Link existence, editorial intent, current availability, and policy risk require separate evidence. Do not infer authority from counts alone.

<a id="site-reputation-abuse"></a>
## Site reputation abuse

Evaluate ownership, oversight, purpose, audience value, and commercial relationships before concluding that third-party content is acceptable.

<a id="privacy-consent"></a>
## Privacy and consent

Technical consent diagnostics are not legal determinations. Preserve regional configuration, observed signals, missing evidence, retention, and legal-review requirements.

<a id="claims-compliance"></a>
## Claims and compliance

Material claims require traceable support. Marketing intent does not override source quality, jurisdiction, disclosures, or platform policies.
