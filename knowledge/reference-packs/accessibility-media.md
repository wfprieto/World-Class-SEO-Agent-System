<a id="accessibility"></a>
## Accessibility

Use WCAG as the normative accessibility framework. Automated checks are partial evidence and do not replace keyboard, screen-reader, contrast, labeling, or task-completion review.

<a id="image-seo"></a>
## Image SEO

Use crawlable image delivery, descriptive context, appropriate alt text, stable URLs, supported metadata, and performance controls. Avoid keyword stuffing and decorative-alt misuse.

<a id="video-transcripts"></a>
## Video and transcripts

Separate media discoverability, transcript structure, caption accuracy, speaker identity, and legal accessibility requirements. Structural checks do not verify spoken-word accuracy.

<a id="ai-media-provenance"></a>
## AI media provenance

Preserve source and edit provenance where supported. Labeling metadata is not copyright ownership, authenticity proof, or publication authorization.
