## Local SEO

Resolve business identity, location or service-area scope, and source tier before comparing NAP or local landing pages. LocalBusiness markup must match visible facts.

<a id="ecommerce-merchant"></a>
## Ecommerce and merchant data

Coordinate site structure, product data, structured data, URL design, reviews, pagination, and merchant feeds. Provider and feed states remain distinct from page evidence.

<a id="programmatic-seo"></a>
## Programmatic SEO

Scaled pages require user value, unique decision-useful information, crawl and index controls, monitoring, and rollback. Avoid doorway patterns and fabricated local relevance.

<a id="faceted-navigation"></a>
## Faceted navigation

Control crawlable combinations, canonical strategy, internal linking, and index eligibility. Do not apply one rule blindly across all inventory and demand patterns.
