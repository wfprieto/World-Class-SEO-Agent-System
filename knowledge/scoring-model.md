# Scoring Model

Use this default model for full audits. Adjust weights for business type.

| Pillar | Weight |
| --- | ---: |
| Technical SEO | 18 |
| Content quality and intent | 16 |
| Information architecture and internal linking | 12 |
| Authority, PR, and backlink health | 10 |
| UX, accessibility, and CRO | 12 |
| Local or international fit | 8 |
| GEO/AIO and entity readiness | 10 |
| Measurement, analytics, and drift monitoring | 8 |
| Compliance and security | 6 |

## Score Rules

- A score without evidence is provisional.
- Missing first-party data lowers confidence, not necessarily the score.
- Critical risks cap the total score at 79 until resolved.
- Sitewide indexation defects cap the total score at 69 until resolved.
- Security or manual-action risks cap the total score at 59 until resolved.
