# SEO Quality Gates

## Universal Gate

Every recommendation must include:

- Evidence
- Confidence
- Impact
- Effort
- Risk
- Owner
- Acceptance criteria
- Verification method

## Technical SEO Gate

- Valuable pages are crawlable.
- Valuable pages are indexable.
- Canonicals are intentional.
- Redirects are clean.
- XML sitemaps include canonical valuable URLs.
- robots.txt does not block important resources or pages by accident.
- Structured data describes visible content.
- Mobile rendering works.
- Core Web Vitals issues are triaged with field data when available.
- Core Web Vitals review must explicitly evaluate LCP, INP, and CLS. Do not use FID as the current interaction metric.

## Content Gate

- Content satisfies a real user intent.
- Content adds information gain.
- Content has clear authorship or ownership where appropriate.
- Claims are supportable.
- Metadata is useful and accurate.
- Internal links help users.
- Page is not a low-value duplicate.

## GEO/AIO Gate

- Page is eligible for normal search discovery.
- Important answers are clear and concise.
- Entities are consistent.
- Sources and citations are visible where appropriate.
- Passage structure supports extraction without becoming robotic.
- AI visibility claims are dated and measured.

## Accessibility Gate

- Heading order is logical.
- Images have appropriate alt text or empty decorative alt.
- Forms have labels.
- Interactive elements are keyboard accessible.
- Focus state is visible.
- Contrast is sufficient.
- Videos have captions or transcripts where required.

## Local SEO Gate

- NAP is consistent.
- GBP information matches site.
- Location pages are unique and useful.
- Reviews are handled ethically.
- LocalBusiness schema matches real business information.

## International SEO Gate

- Hreflang codes are valid.
- Return tags exist.
- Canonicals align with language-region targets.
- x-default is intentional.
- Translations are localized and reviewed.

## Compliance Gate

- No spam tactics.
- Disclosures are present where needed.
- High-stakes content has expert review.
- Privacy/cookie requirements are escalated.
- Claims are not misleading.
- AI-generated or AI-assisted content is disclosed when required by law, platform policy, client policy, or user trust context.
- AI-assisted content must still meet the same originality, accuracy, authorship, and accountability standards as human-written content.

## Regional Search Gate

- International work identifies dominant search engines and platforms by target market.
- Regional search guidance is validated for Bing, Baidu, Yandex, Naver, or other market-specific platforms when relevant.
- Localization quality is reviewed by market-appropriate expertise for business-critical pages.
