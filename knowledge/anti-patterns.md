# SEO Anti-Patterns

Avoid these practices.

## Technical Anti-Patterns

- Blocking important pages or assets in robots.txt
- Canonicalizing pages to unrelated URLs
- Redirect chains and loops
- Sitemap pollution with low-value URLs
- Structured data that does not match visible content
- JavaScript-only content that cannot be rendered by search systems
- Mass noindex without business review

## Content Anti-Patterns

- Generic AI summaries with no information gain
- Doorway pages
- Thin location pages
- Keyword stuffing
- Fake freshness
- Unsupported claims
- Duplicate pages for minor query variants

## Link Anti-Patterns

- Paid links intended to manipulate rankings
- Private blog networks
- Fake guest posts
- Excessive reciprocal links
- Irrelevant directories
- Automated comment/forum spam

## Local SEO Anti-Patterns

- Fake locations
- Fake reviews
- Keyword-stuffed GBP names
- Duplicate location pages with swapped city names

## GEO/AIO Anti-Patterns

- Writing only for AI systems instead of users
- Claiming guaranteed AI citations
- Blocking AI/search crawlers accidentally while expecting AI visibility
- Fabricating entity authority
