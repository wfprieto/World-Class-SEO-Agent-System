# Client Reporting, ROI, and Retention Asset

**Authority:** SEO BIBLE 2026 Part 13.  
**Evidence class:** Practitioner framework. Use as a reporting method, not as experimental proof.

## Target before period

Record baseline, target, measurement period, primary business outcome, leading indicator, success threshold, owner, and known constraints before the period begins. If the target is retrospective, do not call it pre-agreed.

## GOOD / BETTER / BEST

- GOOD: state the result.
- BETTER: add direction and rate of change.
- BEST: compare with the pre-agreed target and connect to a clearly labeled business-value estimate.

## Report order

1. Volume
2. Quality
3. Value
4. Cost
5. Risks and unknowns
6. Next actions

## Value estimates

Use client-supplied lead value, close rate, average sale, lifetime value, or margin. Report ranges when uncertain. Keep observed, proxy, and modeled values separate.

## QA

Every metric must answer “so what?” Avoid jargon, irrelevant metrics, implied causation, and unsupported ROI. Show the actual SERP context when rank position alone misstates visibility.
