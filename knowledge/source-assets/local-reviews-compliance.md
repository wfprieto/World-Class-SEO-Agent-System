# Local SEO and Review Compliance Asset

**Authority:** SEO BIBLE 2026 Part 11 and its stated limitations.  
**Boundary:** This is policy-risk screening, not legal advice and not proof that reviews directly cause local rankings.

## Prohibited or high-risk practices

- Paying for reviews
- Incentivizing reviews without platform-specific verification
- Owner, employee, or competitor self-review
- Posting on behalf of customers
- Shared in-location review kiosks
- Review gating or deceptive selective solicitation

## Operating workflow

1. Ask eligible customers neutrally after service.
2. Let customers use their own devices and accounts.
3. Respond consistently, with priority on unresolved negatives.
4. Track whether the problem was resolved and whether the review was voluntarily updated.
5. Verify each platform's current rules and the applicable jurisdiction before execution.

## Reporting

Separate review volume, recency, response coverage, sentiment, recovery, and representation accuracy. Do not claim direct ranking causation from the source corpus.
