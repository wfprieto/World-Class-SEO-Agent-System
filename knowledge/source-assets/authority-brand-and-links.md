# Authority, Brand, and Link Intelligence Asset

**Authority:** SEO BIBLE 2026 Parts 1.1, 1.2, 2, 3.6, 7, 8.9, 8.11, and 9.7.  
**Evidence rule:** Do not universalize one study's link-count range. Provider authority metrics remain provider-specific.

## Link evaluation

Evaluate editorial intent, topical relevance, source authority, qualified referral potential, anchor naturalness, placement, follow/nofollow/sponsored/ugc state, and whether the link would remain valuable if search engines disappeared.

## Internal links

Use graph evidence, not a fixed quota. Identify orphans, crawl depth, weak in-degree on commercial pages, links through redirects, links to noncanonical URLs, inaccessible JavaScript navigation, anchor concentration, and missing contextual relationships.

The 23-million-link study is a large observational signal, not a universal rule that every page needs 40 to 50 links.

## Brand corpus

Audit how third-party sources represent the brand across reviews, communities, publications, listicles, podcasts, datasets, partners, marketplaces, and professional associations. Classify gaps as content, product, operations, reputation, entity clarity, or third-party coverage problems.

SEO must not be presented as capable of hiding a genuine product or service defect.
