# Measurement and Attribution Asset

**Authority:** SEO BIBLE 2026 Parts 7, 8.13, 9.8, and 13.  
**Evidence rule:** Correlation is not causation; assisted influence and last-click conversion are different observations.

## Primary measures

- Business conversions and qualified leads
- Organic commercial-page performance
- Branded search demand
- Indexing defects
- Field Core Web Vitals
- AI referrals and dated citation observations
- Cost, time, and human correction required

## Attribution discipline

State causal distance. SEO directly affects technical eligibility and visibility evidence more than it directly affects revenue. Website design, offer quality, sales process, pricing, and operations can mediate conversion.

Where outcomes occur offline, use client-supplied estimates and label the result modeled. Do not treat no-referrer traffic as proven AI influence.

## Metric cautions

- GA4 bounce rate is not Universal Analytics bounce rate.
- Scroll events and engagement require implementation-context review.
- Domain or page authority metrics are comparative third-party metrics, not Google ranking signals.
- Rank position must be interpreted with ads, local packs, AI answers, and other SERP features.
