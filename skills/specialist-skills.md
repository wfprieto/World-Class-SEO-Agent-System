# Specialist SEO Skills

## `local-seo-audit`

Purpose: Evaluate local visibility foundations.

Output:

- GBP readiness
- NAP consistency
- Citation gaps
- Local page quality
- Review health
- Local schema status

Quality gate:

- Reject fake locations, fake reviews, and doorway city pages.

## `hreflang-audit`

Purpose: Validate international targeting.

Output:

- Hreflang inventory
- Return-tag issues
- x-default status
- Canonical conflicts
- Locale coverage gaps

Quality gate:

- Canonical and hreflang signals must not conflict.

## `image-seo-audit`

Purpose: Improve image discoverability, accessibility, and performance.

Output:

- Alt text issues
- File format and size issues
- Responsive image issues
- CLS risk
- Image schema recommendations

Quality gate:

- Alt text must describe image purpose, not stuff keywords.

## `video-seo-audit`

Purpose: Improve video discoverability.

Output:

- Video metadata review
- Transcript needs
- Timestamp plan
- VideoObject schema
- Video sitemap requirements

Quality gate:

- Transcripts and timestamps must be accurate.

## `geo-aio-citation-audit`

Purpose: Evaluate and improve AI search citability.

Output:

- AI citation footprint
- Query/citation gap map
- Passage-level rewrite plan
- Entity and schema recommendations
- AI visibility monitoring plan

Quality gate:

- Mark all AI search snapshots with date, location, and method.

## `knowledge-graph-sync`

Purpose: Align brand, author, product, local, and topic entities across site and external profiles.

Output:

- Entity inventory
- sameAs map
- Schema improvements
- Brand consistency issues

Quality gate:

- Do not fabricate external authority.

## `backlink-profile`

Purpose: Review backlink health and risk.

Output:

- Authority distribution
- Anchor distribution
- Growth/decay trend
- Toxic candidates for human review

Quality gate:

- Never auto-disavow.

## `backlink-gap`

Purpose: Find legitimate authority opportunities competitors have and the site lacks.

Output:

- Prospect list
- Relevance score
- Outreach angle
- Asset requirement

Quality gate:

- Exclude manipulative, irrelevant, paid, or low-trust opportunities.
