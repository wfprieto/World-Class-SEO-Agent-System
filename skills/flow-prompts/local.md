# FLOW Prompts — Local

The FLOW loop applied to Business Profile, local landing pages, and map visibility. Never recommend fake locations, fake reviews, or keyword-stuffed business names.

## GBP categories

```text
For [business type] in [city], recommend the primary Google Business Profile category and the
secondary categories that match services actually offered. Justify each from the service list I
provide. Do not add categories the business cannot genuinely fulfill.
```

## GBP services

```text
From the supplied service list, write clear Business Profile service entries with plain, accurate
descriptions in the customer's language. No keyword stuffing. Flag any service that implies a
regulated claim for compliance review.
```

## GBP description (variants)

```text
Write three Business Profile description options for [business] in [city]. Each: accurate, plain,
specific to what the business does and who it serves, within the character limit, no hype, no
guarantees unless supplied and verifiable. Lead with what the customer gets.
```

## Title tag and meta description (local)

```text
For [local page] targeting [service] + [city], write a title tag (under ~60 chars) and meta
description (under ~155 chars) that match local intent, include the service and location naturally,
and state the value or next step. No stuffing, no fabricated superlatives.
```

## Local homepage / service page rewrite

```text
Rewrite [homepage or service page] for a local audience in [city]. Structure: what you do, who
you serve, proof (reviews/credentials/service area), and the next action (call, book, directions).
Keep NAP consistent with the Business Profile. Buyer language first. Mark any claim needing proof.
```

## Local deep-research brief

```text
Build a local SEO research brief for [business] in [market]: the competitors in the map area, the
review and category patterns that win locally, the service-area logic, and the citation and page
gaps to close first. Use supplied evidence; label inferences. Recommend the single highest-leverage
local action.
```
