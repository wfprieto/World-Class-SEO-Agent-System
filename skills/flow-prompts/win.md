# FLOW Prompts — Win

Use when traffic exists but business impact is weak. Goal: bottom-funnel pages and measurement that connect discovery to revenue. Route pricing, guarantee, and regulated claims to compliance before publishing.

## BOFU page brief

```text
Create a bottom-funnel page brief for [offer] targeting [audience] at [buying stage].
Use only the supplied inputs (objections, customer language, proof points, conversion goal).
Build around the visitor's decision:
1. The immediate problem and the decision risk.
2. The objections in the buyer's words, and the proof that resolves each.
3. The page structure: promise, proof, offer specifics, objection handling, single clear next action.
4. The trust signals required (evidence, credentials, disclosures).
5. The conversion event to measure.
No invented proof. Flag any claim needing legal/compliance review.
```

## Conversion audit

```text
Audit [URL] as a conversion page. Identify where the value proposition, proof, and next action
are unclear or buried. Check CTA-intent match, above-the-fold clarity, and objection coverage.
Return prioritized changes with the expected decision each change unblocks and how to measure it.
Do not promise conversion-rate lifts as facts; frame them as hypotheses to test.
```

## Dual-surface content scorecard

```text
Score [URL] on two surfaces at once. Search surface: intent match, extraction readiness, entity
clarity, internal linking. Business surface: clarity of the offer, proof strength, objection
handling, and the next qualified action. Output a scorecard with the weakest dimension named and
the one change that would move the business outcome most. Tie every claim to supplied evidence.
```
