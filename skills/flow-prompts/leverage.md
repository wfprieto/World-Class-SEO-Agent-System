# FLOW Prompts — Leverage

Use when the brand is not corroborated off-site. Goal: build distributed evidence and authority that both search engines and AI answer engines can find. Recommend only ethical, policy-safe tactics; never manipulative link schemes.

## Off-site authority and mention strategy

```text
Act as a digital PR and authority strategist for [brand] in [market]. Using the supplied
evidence (current backlinks, brand mentions, competitor coverage), produce:
1. The entities and publications where a credible brand in this space is expected to appear.
2. Unlinked-mention and citation opportunities to pursue first.
3. Linkable-asset ideas grounded in our real data or expertise (not generic listicles).
4. A corroboration checklist: Wikipedia/Wikidata, industry directories, reputable reviews,
   community references (Reddit/YouTube/LinkedIn) where genuinely relevant.
Reject link buying, PBNs, and reciprocal schemes. Flag anything that risks a spam-policy issue.
```

## Backlink competition read

```text
From the supplied competitor backlink evidence, identify the referring domains that account
for most of their authority, the asset types those links point to, and the realistic subset we
could earn with a comparable asset. Output: a prioritized target list with the earning angle for
each, and an honest note on which are out of reach. Referring-domain quality over raw count.
Do not present estimated metrics as measured.
```
