# Plain-Language SEO Report: example.com SEO improvements

Lead agent: SEO Output Report Agent

## Quick Summary

We reviewed the website to find issues that could make it harder for search engines and people to understand the site. The biggest opportunities were cleaning up the sitemap, improving internal links, refreshing older content, and making important pages easier to understand.

## What We Looked At

- A sample website crawl
- Search performance data provided for this example
- Page titles and descriptions
- Sitemap and canonical signals
- Content quality and internal links

## What Was Fixed or Improved

| Item | What Changed | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sitemap cleanup | Removed example redirected URLs from the sitemap plan | Search engines get a cleaner list of important pages |
| Internal links | Added recommendations for hub pages to link to important guides | Visitors and search engines can find useful pages more easily |

## What Was Created or Added

| Item | What Was Added | Why It Helps |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Content refresh plan | A plan for updating older articles | Keeps important pages accurate and useful |
| Technical ticket | A developer-ready sitemap fix ticket | Makes the fix easier to complete correctly |

## Content and Copy Updates

The recommended copy updates focus on making important pages clearer, more helpful, and less generic. The content should answer the reader's question quickly and include useful details competitors are missing.

## Meta Tag and Search Appearance Updates

The metadata recommendations improve how pages may appear in search results. These changes help people understand what each page is about before clicking.

## Important Findings

| Finding | What It Means in Plain English | Priority |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sitemap includes redirected URLs | Some listed pages point somewhere else instead of loading directly | High |
| Older content needs refreshing | Some useful pages may look outdated or incomplete | Medium |

## Recommendations

| Recommendation | Why We Recommend It | Suggested Timing |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Clean the sitemap | Helps search engines focus on the right pages | Next development cycle |
| Refresh older content | Helps visitors trust the information | Next content sprint |
| Add clearer internal links | Helps people move through the site | Within 30 days |

## What Still Needs Attention

The site should be checked again after the sitemap and content updates are complete.

## What We Could Not Fully Check

Server log data and real conversion data were not available in this example.

## Next Steps

1. Clean the sitemap.
2. Refresh the highest-priority older pages.
3. Recheck search performance after the next crawl cycle.

## Simple Glossary

| Term | Plain-English Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Sitemap | A file that lists pages you want search engines to discover |
| Meta description | A short page summary that may appear in search results |
| Canonical | A signal that tells search engines which version of a page is the main one |

## Important Note

SEO improvements help search engines and people better understand and use the website, but rankings, traffic, and leads cannot be guaranteed. Results should be measured over time.
